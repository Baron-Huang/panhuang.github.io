About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research interests include Computational Pathology, Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), Medical Vision-Language Models (MLLMs), Multi-Agent Systems, and Digital Pathology.

During my Ph.D. and postdoctoral training, I have published 25 SCI JCR-Q1 papers as first or corresponding author in venues including Information Fusion, IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging (TMI), Medical Image Analysis (MedIA), IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI), IEEE Journal of Automatica Sinica (JAS), and MICCAI.
